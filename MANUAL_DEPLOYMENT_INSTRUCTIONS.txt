MANUAL FIREBASE DEPLOYMENT INSTRUCTIONS

1. OPEN FIREBASE CONSOLE:
   - Go to https://console.firebase.google.com/
   - Sign in with your Firebase account

2. SELECT YOUR PROJECT:
   - Select the project: fees-management-system-70bb7

3. ENABLE HOSTING:
   - In the left sidebar, click "Hosting"
   - Click "Get started" if you haven't enabled it yet

4. MANUAL DEPLOYMENT:
   - In the Hosting section, click "Upload files"
   - Upload ALL files from the "dist" folder:
     * index.html
     * manifest.json
     * Logo.png
     * assets/ folder with all its contents

5. CONFIGURE REWRITES:
   - In the Hosting configuration, ensure you have a rewrite rule:
     Source: **
     Destination: /index.html

6. DEPLOY:
   - Click "Deploy" to publish your site
   - Your site will be available at: https://fees-management-system-70bb7.firebaseapp.com

TROUBLESHOOTING:
- If you see a blank page, check that all files were uploaded correctly
- Make sure the rewrite rule is configured properly
- Clear your browser cache if changes don't appear immediately
